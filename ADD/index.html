Interesting Facts About Dairy Milk

No. Interesting Fact
1 Dairy Milk was first introduced in 1905 by Cadbury in the United Kingdom.
2 It was one of the first chocolates to contain milk, revolutionizing the chocolate industry.
3 In 2015, Dairy Milk became the first chocolate brand to achieve a Guinness World Record for the largest chocolate bar.
4 Over 40 million Dairy Milk bars are sold every year around the world!
5 Dairy Milk uses 100% sustainably sourced cocoa, helping support farmers worldwide.
